⚠️ Important — Please Read First

Your answers indicate you have experienced symptoms that can be serious. If any of the following occur, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately:

  • Throat swelling or trouble breathing
  • Trouble swallowing or speaking
  • Dizziness or feeling faint during a hive or swelling episode
  • Drooling or inability to swallow your own saliva

Use an epinephrine auto-injector (EpiPen) if you have one, then call 911. Do not wait.
